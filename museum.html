My Work

AR Museum App

The concept of the application for those who like to go to museums and for those who just have to understand how much they miss without going there. The app can offer visitors a variety of services in the palm of their hands. Allows users to find information and buy tickets for famous museums and art places around the world. By using the app users are able to dive into AR world and learn a lot more about the artefacts.

Augmented reality feature allows users to get the most from visiting a museum and make it an unforgettable experience. Visitors use the camera on their phones or tablets to explore the paintings. It works as an virtual guide for people who don't have the time or patience for traditional guided tours, and best of all, they can repeat the tour again and again. The app provides audio and video information for each painting and sculpture.

Before exhibition

The app shows the most visited museum and recommend exhibitions that match users preference. Also they can buy the tickets directly using payment method.

During Exhibition

Users can download AR experience for the museum they are visiting. They only need to turn on the camera and to scan an artefact. The AR quide allows the users to learn more about the lives of artists and the stories behind their work.

First Steps

Users need to be registered in order to continue using the application.

Home Page

On the main page they can always change the city or use the search for a specific request. All museums are dedided into popular categories and recommend places to visit.

Museum Page

The most necessary information for the museum working hours, the ability to rate the museum or share it with friends.

Buying tickets and Payment

To purchase tickets, just select the date, time and type of ticket (children or adult). It's enough to add a card once and make a purchase of tickets in a few seconds to minimize the time of the process of buying tickets. After payment, tickets will be available in the profile.

Augmented reality feature

Scan an artefact with phone's camera and see in an exciting new way the world of arts.
