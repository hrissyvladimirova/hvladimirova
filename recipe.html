My Work

Recipe App

Recipe App is a solution for recipes with ingredients you love. This is a platform where you can make your favourite food within a minute. Every recipe have an augmented reality feature for better and easier experience. Users can save recipes, add them to favourite and even share them with friends.

Main Idea

The app is designed for people who love to cook but struggle with their cooking skills or want to share their cooking tips. The purpose is to motivate them to eat healthy and have a good cooking experince.

Main Goal

A convenient app that offers a nutritions facts and easy recipes and tutorials. A smart cooking application who respects users preferences and their dietary restrictions. It can be potentially used everywhere but will be mostly used at home or where cooking is performed.

Filter meal preferences

Users can filter the cooking time, cuisine type, calories and have an allergen options. They can also add what they want in their recipe

Detailed recipe information

Users scroll through a list of recipes to choose from. They can also see how many people have tried this recipe, an important nutrition facts and cooking time.

Recipe tutorial

Once a recipe has been selected, users enter the main cooking screen, where they are walked through the recipe step by step. They can also watch a video tutorial demonstrating the recipe tehnique.

AR feature

Users can use their phone's camera to guide them through cooking experience At each step, users see a detailed instructions, a help menu and an interactive table of contents laying out all steps in the recipe. This feature saves time by not needing to continually consult the cookbook and minimize mistakes because phones are alerting the users when a timer runs out or they need to go back to a step.
